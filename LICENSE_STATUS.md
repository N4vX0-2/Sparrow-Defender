## Defensive Intent

Sparrow Security Defense Suite is intended solely for defensive cybersecurity purposes, including protection against phishing, credential theft, malicious redirects, deceptive websites, suspicious downloads, and payload-drop attempts.

The project is not intended for offensive use, unauthorized monitoring, credential interception, privacy invasion, malware development, surveillance, or misuse against third-party systems.

## Privacy and Local-First Design

The project is designed around a privacy-conscious, local-first model. Its purpose is to help protect Windows systems and users without turning the defensive tool into a surveillance mechanism.

Future public documentation, if released, will clearly describe what data is processed locally, what optional threat-intelligence integrations may require external lookups, and what controls are available to users or administrators.
