# Project Inquiries

Thank you for your interest in **Sparrow Security Defense Suite**.

Individuals, organizations, researchers, testers, vendors, or prospective users who are interested in learning more about the project, discussing potential collaboration, requesting future updates, or asking about evaluation access should contact:

**sparrow@secured-harbor.com**

Please include a brief description of your interest, organization if applicable, and the reason for your inquiry.

## Current Status

At this time:

- The project is not publicly released.
- Public downloads are not available.
- Public production deployment is not currently supported.
- Access to private builds, demonstrations, or technical details is at the discretion of the project owner.

## Intended Audience

Sparrow Security Defense Suite is being developed for defensive cybersecurity use, including protection against phishing, fake login pages, malicious redirects, suspicious downloads, and payload-drop attempts on Windows systems.
